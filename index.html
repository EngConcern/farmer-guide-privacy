Farmer Guide - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Farmer Guide is developed and operated by Havano ERP, located in Harare, Zimbabwe. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Farmer Guide mobile application (available on Google Play).

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Farmer Guide app, we may collect the following information:

  • Registration Information: Name, phone number, email address, province, district, ward, village, sex, and age bracket — provided by you during account registration.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and a unique device identifier — collected automatically to improve app performance and support.
  • Location Data: Your approximate or precise location (with your permission) to provide localized weather forecasts and agricultural information relevant to your area.
  • Photos: Images you choose to upload when posting products on the marketplace. We only access photos you explicitly select; we do not access your full photo library.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To send you a one-time verification code (OTP) via email for account verification and login.
  • To provide localized weather information and agricultural resources based on your location.
  • To enable you to list and browse products on the marketplace.
  • To send you notifications about relevant agricultural updates (with your permission).
  • To improve the app's functionality and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use secure cloud hosting services to store and process your data. These providers are bound by data protection agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes.
  • Marketplace Listings: Information you include in marketplace product listings (such as product name, description, location, and contact details) is visible to other app users.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Some data (such as your profile and downloaded resources) is stored locally on your device for offline access.

5. Permissions We Request

The Farmer Guide app requests the following device permissions:

  • Location: To provide weather forecasts and location-relevant agricultural data.
  • Camera & Photos: To allow you to take or select photos for marketplace product listings.
  • Internet: To fetch agricultural resources, weather data, and sync your account.
  • Notifications: To alert you about weather updates, download progress, and important agricultural information.

All permissions are optional and requested only when needed. The app remains functional with limited features if permissions are denied.

6. Children's Privacy

The Farmer Guide app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
